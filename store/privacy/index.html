Privacy Policy — Loan Payoff US

Effective date: April 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Loan Payoff US — Debt Calculator ("App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information. By using the App you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Data you provide: Loan inputs (amounts, rates, terms) entered in the calculator. This data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers.

Automatically collected data: We use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymized usage statistics and crash reports (device model, OS version, app version, feature usage events). No personally identifiable information is collected through these services.

Advertising: The free version displays ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. Premium users see no ads.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the App's features
  • To diagnose crashes and fix bugs
  • To understand feature usage in aggregate (no individual tracking)
  • To serve relevant ads to free-tier users

3. Data Storage and Security

All loan calculations are stored locally on your device using SQLite. We do not operate a server-side database of user calculations. Data is protected at rest using Android's built-in storage security. Backup is disabled (android:allowBackup="false"). All network traffic uses TLS; cleartext traffic is blocked by our network security configuration.

4. In-App Purchases

Premium unlock is processed entirely through Google Play Billing. We do not collect or store payment information. Purchase records are managed by Google Play.

5. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Know: You may request information about the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.
  • Right to Delete: You may request deletion of personal information we have collected, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale: We do not sell personal information. AdMob may share data with advertising partners; you can opt out via your device's "Opt out of Ads Personalization" setting.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights.

To exercise your rights, contact us at abassimaj@gmail.com.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

  • Google Play Billing
