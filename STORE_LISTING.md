## English

### Title (max 30 chars)
Loan Payoff Calculator US

### Short Description (max 80 chars)
See exactly how extra payments slash your loan interest & payoff date.

### Long Description (max 4000 chars)
Loan Payoff Calculator US is the most precise extra payment tool on Android. Stop guessing what a little extra each month does for your debt — see the exact dollar savings and months shaved off your loan, instantly.

**What you can calculate:**
- Payoff date for your current loan balance and payment schedule
- Impact of any extra payment amount — monthly, one-time lump sum, or both
- Total interest saved compared to your original loan terms
- Number of months (or years) eliminated from your repayment timeline
- Side-by-side comparison: original payoff vs. accelerated payoff

**Why Loan Payoff Calculator US is different:**
Most debt apps show you a balance. This app shows you the real cost of inaction — and the real reward of every extra dollar you put toward your principal. Enter $50/month extra and see exactly: $1,847 saved in interest, 14 months sooner. That's the kind of clarity that changes behavior.

The "extra payment impact" visualization makes the savings tangible and immediate, turning an abstract goal into a concrete plan.

**Key features:**
- Bilingual: English and Spanish — full interface switch, no restart
- Export your payoff plan to PDF and share with a financial advisor or partner
- Save multiple payoff scenarios to history and compare strategies
- Works for any loan type: auto, personal, student, or other installment loans
- No account required, no internet connection needed — 100% private and local
- Clean, fast interface designed for quick what-if analysis

**Who this is for:**
Loan Payoff Calculator US is for anyone with a loan who wants to get out of debt faster. Whether you're working on a car loan, a personal loan, or any fixed-term debt, this tool shows you exactly what your options are — without the guesswork.

**Privacy:** All calculations run locally on your device. No personal or financial data is ever transmitted or stored outside your phone.
